Neural Data Science

Hi! My name is Maria. I am currently doing a self-study MSc thesis on calcium imaging and Neuropixel probe data analysis, focusing on mouse vision. Doing data analysis massive Allen Brain Observatory data and combining it with HuggingFace Transformers pushed me to start developing my own data analysis software, which I started during the BackdropBuild event. In my day job, I'm a machine learning engineer working on applying Natural Language Processing algorithms (ie Transformers) to legal and police data.
The images above show the sensitivity pattern to stimuli in the visual space of groups of neurons in the primary visual cortex (V1) of the mouse. Images that the mice were viewing were regressed against these neural activity components to visualize the ensemble receptive fields of cortical cells.
